SHEETS: 2.Rank by Brand / 3.ICE by Brand / 4.BEV by Brand / 5.HEV by Brand / 6.PHEV by Brand
SOURCE FILE: mastercal.xlsx
PURPOSE: Monthly registration by brand, one sheet per powertrain filter

==============================================================
SHEET DEFINITIONS
==============================================================
Sheet               Title                           Powertrain filter
------------------------------------------------------------------
2.Rank by Brand     Registration by Brand           ALL (no filter)
3.ICE by Brand      ICE Registration by Brand       ICE only
4.BEV by Brand      BEV Registration by Brand       BEV only
5.HEV by Brand      HEV Registration by Brand       HEV only
6.PHEV by Brand     PHEV Registration by Brand      PHEV only

All 5 sheets share the SAME column layout. Only the data rows differ.

==============================================================
ROW LAYOUT (header block)
==============================================================
Row 1:  Title string (e.g. "Registration by Brand")
Row 2:  (blank)
Row 3:  "ประเภทรถ : รย.1,2,3,6,9,10,11"  | col B = "(Multiple Items)"
Row 4:  "Powertrain : ALL" (or "ICE" / "BEV" etc.)  | col B = filter value
Row 5:  (blank)
Row 6:  (blank — sheets 3-6 have "Sum of จำนวนรถ" here, ignore it)
Row 7:  year span labels  (col B = prev year, col S = curr year)
Row 8:  month sub-labels  (Jan Feb Mar Apr May ... Jun Jul ... Dec | Total | Jan Feb Mar Apr May)
Row 9:  Units/Share labels
Row 10: Grand Total data row
Row 11+: brand rows, sorted by curr month units descending

==============================================================
COLUMN LAYOUT  (example: curr month = May)
==============================================================
Col  A : Brand (ยี่ห้อรถ2)
Col  B : prev year Jan  (Units)
Col  C : prev year Feb  (Units)
Col  D : prev year Mar  (Units)
Col  E : prev year Apr  (Units)
Col  F : prev year [curr month] Units
Col  G : prev year [curr month] Share
Col  H : prev year Jan–[curr month] YTD Units
Col  I : prev year Jan–[curr month] YTD Share
Col  J : prev year [curr month+1]   e.g. Jun
Col  K : prev year [curr month+2]   e.g. Jul
Col  L : prev year [curr month+3]   e.g. Aug
Col  M : prev year [curr month+4]   e.g. Sep
Col  N : prev year [curr month+5]   e.g. Oct
Col  O : prev year [curr month+6]   e.g. Nov
Col  P : prev year [curr month+7]   e.g. Dec
Col  Q : prev year Full Year Units
Col  R : prev year Full Year Share
Col  S : curr year Jan
Col  T : curr year Feb
Col  U : curr year Mar
Col  V : curr year Apr
Col  W : curr year [curr month] Units
Col  X : curr year [curr month] Share
Col  Y : Share Diff  = curr month share - prev year curr month share
Col  Z : MoM growth  = (curr month - prev month curr year) / prev month curr year
Col AA : YoY growth  = (curr month curr year - curr month prev year) / curr month prev year
Col AB : curr year Jan–[curr month] YTD Units

NOTE: Stop at col AB. Template has extra formula columns beyond this — do not touch.

==============================================================
DATA ROWS
==============================================================
Row 10: Grand Total  (sum of all brands in the filtered set)
Row 11+: One row per brand, sorted by curr year curr month Units DESCENDING
         Brands with 0 across all months can be omitted

==============================================================
PARQUET COLUMNS USED
==============================================================
ปี, เดือน, ประเภทรถ, ยี่ห้อรถ2, Powertrain, จำนวนรถ
Filter: ประเภทรถ matches รย.(1|2|3|6|9|10|11)
Filter: Powertrain = [sheet's powertrain] or ALL

==============================================================
CALCULATION RULES
==============================================================
- Share    = row value / Grand Total (same col)
- Diff     = curr_month_curr_year_share - curr_month_prev_year_share
- MoM      = (curr_month_curr_year - prev_month_curr_year) / prev_month_curr_year
             None if January or prev month = 0
- YoY      = (curr_month_curr_year - curr_month_prev_year) / curr_month_prev_year
             None if prev year value = 0
- YTD      = Jan through curr month sum
- Missing period → None (blank), never 0
