SHEET: 7.BEV by Model
SOURCE FILE: mastercal.xlsx
PURPOSE: BEV Major registration broken down by Brand → Model hierarchy
FILTER: ประเภทรถ = รย.1,2,3,6,9,10,11 | Powertrain = "BEV Major" only

==============================================================
ROW LAYOUT (header block)
==============================================================
Row 1:  "Registration of Major BEV Brand by Model"
Row 2:  (blank)
Row 3:  "ประเภทรถ : รย.1,2,3,6,9,10,11"  | col B = "(Multiple Items)"
Row 4:  "Powertrain : BEV (only Major BEV Brand)"  | col B = "BEV Major"
Row 5:  (blank)
Row 6:  year span labels  (col A = "Brand/Model", col B = prev year, col S = curr year)
Row 7:  month/period sub-labels
Row 8:  Units/Share/Growth labels
Row 9:  Grand Total data row
Row 10+: Brand rows (bold/parent) then Model rows (child) nested under each brand

==============================================================
COLUMN LAYOUT  (example: curr month = May)
==============================================================
Col  A : Brand or Model label
         — Brand rows: brand name (ยี่ห้อรถ2)
         — Model rows: model name (รุ่นรถ2)
Col  B : prev year Jan  (Units)
Col  C : prev year Feb  (Units)
Col  D : prev year Mar  (Units)
Col  E : prev year Apr  (Units)
Col  F : prev year [curr month] Units
Col  G : prev year [curr month] Share  (vs Grand Total)
Col  H : prev year Jan–[curr month] YTD Units
Col  I : prev year Jan–[curr month] YTD Share
Col  J : prev year [curr month+1]  e.g. Jun
Col  K : prev year [curr month+2]  e.g. Jul
Col  L : prev year [curr month+3]  e.g. Aug
Col  M : prev year [curr month+4]  e.g. Sep
Col  N : prev year [curr month+5]  e.g. Oct
Col  O : prev year [curr month+6]  e.g. Nov
Col  P : prev year [curr month+7]  e.g. Dec
Col  Q : prev year Full Year Units
Col  R : prev year Full Year Share
Col  S : curr year Jan
Col  T : curr year Feb
Col  U : curr year Mar
Col  V : curr year Apr
Col  W : curr year [curr month] Units
Col  X : curr year [curr month] Share  (vs Grand Total)
Col  Y : Share Diff  = curr month share (curr year) - curr month share (prev year)
Col  Z : MoM growth  = (curr month - prev month curr year) / prev month curr year
Col AA : YoY growth  = (curr month curr year - curr month prev year) / curr month prev year
Col AB : curr year Jan–[curr month] YTD Units
Col AC : curr year Jan–[curr month] YTD Share
Col AD : YTD Diff
Col AE : YTD YoY growth

NOTE: Stop at col AE. Do not touch columns beyond this.

==============================================================
DATA ROW STRUCTURE (hierarchical)
==============================================================
Row 9:   Grand Total  (all BEV Major brands combined)
Row 10:  Brand A      (e.g. BYD)      ← brand-level subtotal
Row 11:  Model A1     (e.g. BYD DOLPHIN)
Row 12:  Model A2     (e.g. BYD ATTO 3)
Row 13:  Model A3     (e.g. BYD SEALION 7)
...      (all models for Brand A)
Row N:   Brand B      (e.g. MG)
Row N+1: Model B1     (e.g. MG ZS EV)
...      etc.

Sort order:
- Brands sorted by curr year curr month Units DESCENDING
- Models within each brand sorted by curr year curr month Units DESCENDING

==============================================================
SHARE REFERENCE
==============================================================
- Brand share: brand curr month / Grand Total curr month
- Model share: model curr month / Brand curr month  (parent brand, not Grand Total)

==============================================================
PARQUET COLUMNS USED
==============================================================
ปี, เดือน, ประเภทรถ, ยี่ห้อรถ2, รุ่นรถ2, Powertrain, จำนวนรถ
Filter: ประเภทรถ matches รย.(1|2|3|6|9|10|11)
Filter: Powertrain == "BEV Major"

==============================================================
CALCULATION RULES
==============================================================
- Brand Share  = brand value / Grand Total value (same col)
- Model Share  = model value / parent brand value (same col)
- Diff         = curr share - prev year same period share
- MoM          = (curr month - prev month curr year) / prev month curr year
- YoY          = (curr month curr year - curr month prev year) / curr month prev year
- YTD          = Jan through curr month sum
- Missing period → None (blank), never 0
