SHEET: 8.Rank by BEV Model
SOURCE FILE: mastercal.xlsx
PURPOSE: Ranking table of individual BEV Major models by registration volume
FILTER: ประเภทรถ = รย.1,2,3,6,9,10,11 | Powertrain = "BEV Major" only

==============================================================
ROW LAYOUT (header block)
==============================================================
Row 1:  "Ranking of Registration of Major BEV Models"
Row 2:  (blank)
Row 3:  "ประเภทรถ : รย.1,2,3,6,9,10,11"
Row 4:  "Powertrain : BEV (only Major BEV Brand)"
Row 5:  (blank)
Row 6:  (blank)
Row 7:  year span labels  (col A = "Model", col B = "Brand", col C = prev year, col T = curr year, col AH = "Rank")
Row 8:  month/period sub-labels
Row 9:  Units/Share/Growth/Rank labels
Row 10: Grand Total data row
Row 11+: one row per BEV model, sorted by curr year curr month Units DESCENDING

==============================================================
COLUMN LAYOUT  (example: curr month = May)
==============================================================
Col  A : Model (รุ่นรถ2)
Col  B : Brand (ยี่ห้อรถ2)
Col  C : prev year Jan  (Units)
Col  D : prev year Feb  (Units)
Col  E : prev year Mar  (Units)
Col  F : prev year Apr  (Units)
Col  G : prev year [curr month] Units
Col  H : prev year [curr month] Share  (vs Grand Total)
Col  I : prev year Jan–[curr month] YTD Units
Col  J : prev year Jan–[curr month] YTD Share
Col  K : prev year [curr month+1]  e.g. Jun
Col  L : prev year [curr month+2]  e.g. Jul
Col  M : prev year [curr month+3]  e.g. Aug
Col  N : prev year [curr month+4]  e.g. Sep
Col  O : prev year [curr month+5]  e.g. Oct
Col  P : prev year [curr month+6]  e.g. Nov
Col  Q : prev year [curr month+7]  e.g. Dec
Col  R : prev year Full Year Units
Col  S : prev year Full Year Share
Col  T : curr year Jan
Col  U : curr year Feb
Col  V : curr year Mar
Col  W : curr year Apr
Col  X : curr year [curr month] Units
Col  Y : curr year [curr month] Share
Col  Z : Share Diff  = curr year share - prev year share
Col AA : MoM growth  = (curr month - prev month curr year) / prev month curr year
Col AB : YoY growth  = (curr month curr year - curr month prev year) / curr month prev year
Col AC : curr year Jan–[curr month] YTD Units
Col AD : curr year Jan–[curr month] YTD Share
Col AE : YTD Diff
Col AF : YTD YoY growth
--- Rank columns ---
Col AH : Rank in prev year  (rank by curr month prev year, 1 = highest)
Col AI : Rank in curr year  (rank by curr month curr year, 1 = highest)
Col AJ : Rank Diff = prev year rank - curr year rank  (positive = improved)

NOTE: Grand Total row has no rank values. Rank cols only apply to model rows.

==============================================================
DATA ROWS
==============================================================
Row 10: Grand Total
Row 11+: one row per unique (รุ่นรถ2, ยี่ห้อรถ2) combination
         sorted by curr year curr month Units DESCENDING
         Models with 0 in all curr year months still appear if they had prev year volume

==============================================================
RANK CALCULATION
==============================================================
- Rank in prev year  = rank of model by [curr month prev year] units, among all models
                       (1 = most registrations)
- Rank in curr year  = rank of model by [curr month curr year] units
- Rank Diff          = rank_prev - rank_curr  (positive means moved up in ranking)
- Grand Total row: leave rank cols blank

==============================================================
PARQUET COLUMNS USED
==============================================================
ปี, เดือน, ประเภทรถ, ยี่ห้อรถ2, รุ่นรถ2, Powertrain, จำนวนรถ
Filter: ประเภทรถ matches รย.(1|2|3|6|9|10|11)
Filter: Powertrain == "BEV Major"

==============================================================
CALCULATION RULES
==============================================================
- Share    = row value / Grand Total (same col)
- Diff     = curr year share - prev year share (same period)
- MoM      = (curr month - prev month curr year) / prev month curr year
- YoY      = (curr month curr year - curr month prev year) / curr month prev year
- YTD      = sum Jan through curr month
- Missing period → None (blank), never 0
